Burger

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Bun 2
Meat 1
Cheese 1

Step 1: Toast buns in a toaster.
Step 2: Fry meat in a pan with oil.
Step 3: Add cheese ontop of the meat.
Step 4: Once cooked, stack the burger.
